OUR COURSES

Electrical & Electronic Engineering Technology

The programme is designed to train students to qualify craftman in the Electrical Engineering Industry, to be able to work in construction, maintenance and distribution of electrical system. Boys and Girls are equally enrolled in this program.

INFORMATION COMMUNICATION TECHNOLOGY

The department also stands out as a separate department for training in computer Hardware Internet Orientation, Software (MS Word, Excel, Access, Publisher etc) for secretarial and designing, Networking, website design, Graphic Designing, Video editing computer Servicing and maintenance upgrading and installation etc. Boys and Girls are equally enrolled.

AUTO MECHANICAL ENGINEERING TECHNOLOGY

The course has been designed to enable the trainees to acquire skills and knowledge in the reading of instruments. E.g. Calipers, how to measure accurately, filling and drilling holes, setting works in the three or four jaw chucks, cutting of gears, threads on machine tools etc Boys and Girls are equally enrolled.

MECHANICAL ENGINEERING TECHNOLOGY

The department is responsible for training in Mechanical Engineering Craft Practice(Machining and Maintenace Fitting) at the basic level with adequate practice in the use of LATHE Machine, Drilling machine, Milling Machine and all types of cutting machine etc Boys and Girls are equally enrolled.

OIL AND GAS TECHNOLOGY

This programme is designed to create future leaders of the industry and the range of subject taught ensures that student will have a strong appreciation of all key element of the industry life cycle and of the different areas of engineering acquired, as well as how they work today to deliver projects safely and efficiently.

REFRIGERATION & AIR-CONDITIONING TECHNOLOGY

The department seek to train student to demonstrate a great sense of knowledge and skills in the field of refrigeration and air-conditioning technology and develop a close working relationship with the industry of work in order to provide solutions to meet their operations. They are responsible for installation of cooling systems, design, construct and operate cooling systems as well as test, troubleshoot and perform basic repairs all types of cooling systems.
