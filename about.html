ABOUT SEKONDI TAKORADI TECHNICAL INSTITUTE

Our History

The Sekondi-Takorodi Technical Institute(S.T.T.I) previously known and called Takoradi Vocational Training Institute (TVTI) was established on 22nd September, 1998 by His Excellency John Evans Fiifi Atta Mills through the initiative of the government of Ghana in partnership with the Department for International Development(DFID) project. It was established to provide the best employable TVET skills and services to the youth in the region and to raise training to the middle level manpower requirement, to meet the modern technological trend. T.V.T.I was changed to Sekondi Takoradi Technical Institute as a result of the government's Pre-Tertiary Education Act, 2020 (Act 1049) which established Ghana Technical and Vocational Educationa and Training Service (Ghana TVET Service) in the year 2022.



OUR ULTIMATE GOAL FOR OUR STUDENTS

The object of service is to manage, oversee and implement approved national policies and programmes relating to pre-tertiary technical and vocational education and training.With the establishment of the Ghana TVET Service under the Pre-Tertiary Education Act, 2020 (Act 1049), all TVET providing institutions under nine (9) ministries (GES - TVED, DICCES, NVTI, OIC, Social Welfare Training Centre, Youth Leadership Training Institutes, GRATIS, Technology, Government Technical Training Centre, Community Development Vocational Institute) have been realigned to the Ghana TVET Service under Ministry of Education.

HOD FOR INFORMATION AND COMMUNICATION TECHNOLOGY

The deparment is responsible for training trainees of the institue in computer literacy program. The department also stands out as a separate department for training in computer Hardware Internet Orientation, Software (MS Word, Excel, Access, Publisher etc) for secretarial and designing, Networking, website design, Graphic Designing, Video editing computer Servicing and maintenance upgrading and installation etc. Boys and Girls are equally enrolled

ELECTRICAL ENGINEERING TECHNOLOGY

Trainees in this field are trained to specialize in commercial, industrial and domestic electrical wiring systems. They learn how to work on single and three phase electrical systems. They also train in installation, maintenance, repair and commissioning of industrial electrical machines including controls and protection systems. The Department had been in existence from 1998. The programme is designed to train students to qualify as craftsman in the Electrical Engineering Industry, to be able to work in construction, maintenance and distribution of electrical system. Boys and Girls are equally enrolled.

MOTOR VEHICLE TECHNOLOGY (AUTO-MECH)

The department is responsible for training in Mechanical Engineering Craft Practice (Machining & Maintenance Fitting) at the basic level with adequate practical in the use of Lathe machine, drilling machine, milling machine and all types of cutting machine etc. The department has been in existence since 1998. The course has been designed to enable the trainees to acquire skills and knowledge in the reading of instruments. E.g. Calipers, how to measure accurately, filling and drilling holes, setting works in the three or four jaw chucks, cutting of gears, threads on machine tools, turning tapers and heat treatments of metals (materials), general machine, workshop practices, manufacture of simple machine tools, compressors drives hydraulics, pumps and all types of maintenance works. Boys and Girls are equally enrolled.

WELDING AND FABRICATION TECHNOLOGY

This department is responsible for training in Welding and Fabrication. The Welding course covers the following: The theory and practice of welding of sheet and tick plates of metals with both oxyacetylene and the manual metals and welding processes. It also covers metal fabrication and design. The department is responsible for training individuals and organizations in general welding and fabrication, Mig and Tig Welding for oil and gas and all metal structural works. On completion, trainees are expected to be able to read and interpret welding symbols and technical drawing. Trainees must be able to weld in all welding positions and conduct all welding testing required. Boys and Girls are equally enrolled.

CISM DEPARTMENT

Generic skills are responsible for teaching English Language, Mathematics, Entrepreneurship Skill Training (EST), Integrated Science and Information Communication Technology (ICT). These subjects are thought in all the departments. The introduction of these subjects is meant to help our trainees to effectively speak, write, improve numeracy and develop entrepreneurial skills for the world of business. It would also help our graduates who wish to further their course at any Technical University to do so easily by writing Core NABPTEX together with their Elective courses.

AIR-CONDITION DEPARTMENT

The objective of the course is to train applicants to have knowledge in the maintenancea and installation of both Refigeration and Airconditioning units. The department seek to train students to demonstrate a great sense of knowledge and skill in the field of Refrigeration and Airconditioning Technology and a develop a close working relationship with the industry of work in order to provide solution to meet their operations.
